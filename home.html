@venelouis method to learn new languages!

Hi, I'm Venelouis, a software engineer who loves to learn new languages. I have a method that I use to learn new languages. And now I will share it with you!

My method to learn new languages isn't anything from another world: learning a new language is basicaly learn words and make correct sentences! So:

  1. Learn the pronouns.
  2. Learn the most used verbs.
  3. Learn the most used objects.
  4. Learn the most used phrases.
  5. Practice making texts.
  6. After understanding phrases, look for interesting songs to practice speaking and pronunciation.
  7. In the end if you can talk with natives about any subject, and if they can understand you, and you they, then you are practicaly learned almost everything about learning a new language!
